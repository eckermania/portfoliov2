    About Me

    I've worked for organizations ranging from small non-profits to multi-national corporations - predominantly in the international development and social responsibility sectors. After building a set of program evaluation and program management skills for over a decade, I wanted to find a career that allowed for more creativity and an opportunity to amplify social impact through software development. I joined the coding bootcamp at Code Fellows in Seattle in mid-2018 and began my journey as a full-stack JavaScript and Java developer. When I'm not lighting figurative fires, I'm often relaxing beside a literal one with my two dogs and husband.
