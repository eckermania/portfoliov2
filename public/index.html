About Me

I've worked for organizations ranging from small non-profits to multi-national corporations - predominantly in the international development and social responsibility sectors. After building a skillset in program evaluation and program management for over a decade, I wanted to find a career that allowed for more creativity and an opportunity to amplify social impact through software development. I joined the Code Fellows coding bootcamp in Seattle in mid-2018 and began my journey as a full-stack JavaScript developer.
